How It Works

Personalized learning at scale.

1. Free Career Assessment

Start with our Free Career Assessment to identify your strengths and align them with the most suitable program. This personalized evaluation sets the foundation for your successful learning journey.

2. Program Onboarding

Seamlessly transition into your chosen program with our comprehensive onboarding process. We'll equip you with all the tools and resources needed to thrive, whether online or offline.

3. Learning Sprint and retrospective

Engage in focused learning sprints followed by retrospectives to track progress and refine your skills. This iterative approach ensures continuous improvement and mastery of core concepts.

4. Capstone Project

Apply what you've learned by working on a Capstone Project that solves real-world problems. This project showcases your abilities and prepares you for industry challenges.

5. Internship (Available only for full-time offline attendees)

Gain practical experience with our internships, exclusive to full-time offline students. You'll work alongside professionals in a real-world setting, bridging the gap between learning and employment.
